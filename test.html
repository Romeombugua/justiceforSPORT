Our Focus Areas

Law Firm Malpractice

At Justice for Sport, we are committed to having the frustrations within the Sports World heard and not brushed under the rug. We leverage technology, media coverage, and strategic partnerships to create a fairer environment for sportspersons and disrupt the bad legal and advisory firms.

  • Investigation of unfair billing practices
  • Review of legal representation quality
  • Assessment of conflict of interest cases

Frivolous Arguments

Our business revolves around examining past and present cases, focusing on potential areas of GDPR breach, malpractice, over-billing, and abuse of power by firms and their representatives. We take decisive action by filing complaints and pursuing justice.

  • Documentation of unjustified legal proceedings
  • Analysis of excessive litigation
  • Identification of procedural abuse

Banks & Poor Advice

We're investigating cases where footballers believe they've received poor investment advice from their banks. Many players have been pressured into schemes that benefited the banks while being steered away from their own preferred investments.

  • Review of investment recommendations
  • Analysis of fee structures
  • Investigation of mis-sold financial products

False Headlines & Mental Health

We understand the devastating impact of misleading media coverage on athletes' mental health. Our team works to address false headlines and their consequences, ensuring athletes have a voice and avenue for redress.

  • Documentation of false media narratives
  • Support for affected athletes
  • Legal action against defamatory content

Impartiality & Justice

We actively file complaints against Solicitors, Barristers, and Judges who fail to uphold the rule of law. Our judicial system must maintain its integrity, and we ensure that any 'bad actors' are held accountable.

  • Monitoring of judicial conduct
  • Investigation of bias claims
  • Documentation of procedural irregularities

GDPR & Data Protection

We investigate potential breaches of data protection regulations, ensuring that athletes' personal information is handled with the care and security it deserves.

  • Audit of data handling practices
  • Investigation of unauthorized disclosures
  • Review of consent procedures
